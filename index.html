Paradigm Pet Professional

Pets are nature's gift to humanity. It has been scientifically proven that opening our homes and hearts to a pet increases our longevity and improves our overall quality of life as well as the lives of our pets.

At Pet Paradigm Professionals, our mission is to offer resources to help you care for your furry, scaly, feathery, and slimy loved ones.

Our pet experts—or “Pexperts”—have been working with pet owners and professionals alike for the past twelve years.

They offer one-on-one consultations with current and prospective pet owners as well as group presentations designed for veterinary, pet shelter, and pet breeding professionals.

Looking for basic pet care advice for the most common type of pets? Need additional help determining which type of pet is right for you and your family?

We will work with you and provide tailored evidence-based pet care to ensure lifelong health and wellness of your new companion.

Our Values

With more than a decade of hands-on experience working closely with pet owners, Paradigm Pet Professionals brings extensive knowledge, practical expertise, and a strong understanding of the challenges pet owners face. Over the past 12 years, we have developed a deep insight into the unique needs of pets and the people who care for them. Paradigm Pet Professionals further distinguishes itself through certifications in pet health and nutrition, reflecting a strong commitment to staying current with the latest research and industry best practices. This specialized expertise allows us to provide informed guidance, trusted recommendations, and effective solutions that support the overall health and well-being of pets. By continuously expanding our knowledge in pet health and nutrition, we are well equipped to help pet owners make confident decisions about their pets’ care. Our dedication to professional growth ensures that clients can rely on Paradigm Pet Professionals for accurate information, personalized support, and thoughtful recommendations tailored to the specific needs of their beloved companions.

Services

Paradigm Pet Professionals offers personalized pet care support through convenient virtual consultations and presentations. We provide one-on-one guidance for pet owners seeking expert advice on general care or specific concerns. We also offer tailored group presentations that cover a wide range of pet care topics, delivering practical tips and valuable insights to support the health and well-being of pets.
